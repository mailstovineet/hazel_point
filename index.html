Hazel Point is one of the best schools in the area. It has modern infrastructure, with unique furniture and technology. You get taught at your own level and pace. The teachers are nice and the students don't have teachers breathing down their necks every time they go somewhere. I'm saying this as a student, and this is honestly a great school.





Battle of the Books + March Madness

Battle of the Books is a competition where teams of up to five people have to answer questions about 20 books they read. Every group has the same 20 books. Whoever gets the most questions right wins. The competition will be held in May. March Madness: "Restart" was voted as the best book in the Hazel Point library following a 16-book tournament.




Esports

The Linnmar High School has an esports team now. Tryouts for Mario Kart were on March 1. Students can play others players competitively across the state and the country.




Egyptian Tomb

The Hazel Point art teacher is planning to make an Egyptian tomb in the hallway outside of her door. 5th graders are making the mummy inside, and 6th graders are making the sarcophagus.
